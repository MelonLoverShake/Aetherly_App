# 🌫️ Haze App (v0.1)

A smart haze monitoring and planning app built with Expo.

Not just a tracker — this app helps you **understand, predict, and navigate air quality** in real-world scenarios.

---

## ✨ Overview

Haze App goes beyond displaying PSI readings. It introduces intelligent features such as indoor air estimation and route-based haze planning, helping users make safer and more informed decisions.

Whether you're commuting, exercising, or planning your day, this app provides **context-aware air quality insights**.

---

## 🚀 Features (v0.1)

### 🌍 Real-Time Air Quality

* Live PSI / PM2.5 readings
* Location-based updates
* Clean, easy-to-read interface

### 🏠 Indoor Air Quality Estimator *(NEW)*

* Estimates indoor PSI based on outdoor conditions
* Accounts for environmental assumptions (e.g. ventilation, sheltering)
* Helps users understand actual exposure indoors

### 🗺️ Route-Based PSI Planner *(NEW)*

* Plan routes with air quality awareness
* Compare PSI levels across different paths
* Identify **healthier travel routes**, not just fastest ones

### 🔔 Smart Alerts

* Notifications for unhealthy conditions
* Early awareness for sudden haze spikes

### ⚡ Lightweight Experience

* Fast, responsive, and minimal UI
* Built for quick decision-making

---

## 💡 Why This App?

Most haze apps only answer:

> “What is the air quality right now?”

This app also answers:

* “Is it safe to stay indoors?”
* “Which route exposes me to less pollution?”
* “How should I plan my day around haze?”
